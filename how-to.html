About Avia

We are three Stanford students on a mission to make flying more accessible and easier for student pilots. Avia is our solution to provide instant, reliable assistance for pilots during their training journey.

Currently, we're testing this online version to gather feedback and improve our AI responses. Our ultimate goal is to develop an offline version that works without internet service, making it accessible even during flights.

Your feedback is crucial! Please use Avia and let us know your thoughts - every piece of feedback helps us create a better tool for the aviation community.
